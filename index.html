Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is an intriguing story-rich open-world action RPG currently under development. In this game, you awaken as Rover, accompanied by a vibrant group of Resonators, embarking on a journey to reclaim your lost memories and overcome the Lament. The game promises a high degree of freedom, allowing players to explore a richly detailed world. For more information, you can visit the official website or check out the interactive map to discover locations for ascension materials, collectibles, and more.
