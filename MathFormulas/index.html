Coordinate Plane

Distance between two points

The distance between the points A(x₁ , y₁) and B(x₂ , y₂) is given by the following formula:

d(A ,B) = √(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²

Perimeter and Area

To find the perimeter of a figure use the formula for the distance between two points.
