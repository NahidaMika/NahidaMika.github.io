Distance between two points

The distance between the points A(x₁ , y₁) and B(x₂ , y₂) is given by the following formula:

d(A ,B) = √(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²

Ratio of a Line Segment

The point P(x,y) that divides a line segment in a given ratio called the division point and is calculated with the formula.

r= P₁P/PP₂

If the distance P₁P₂, is established as positive and the point P is between the points, the distance P₁P and PP₂, will be positive and also the ratio will be positive.

If the point P is outside the point, one of the distances P₁P and PP₂, will be negative, so the ratio r will be negative.
